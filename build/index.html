Projects

The Stinging Fly

For 20 years, The Stinging Fly has been at the forefront of Irish literature. They discover new voices, and help them reach an audience eager to hear their words. To mark their twentieth anniversary, we designed a brand new website for the magazine, built an improved online store, and digitized all of their peerless archive.

Fallow Media

Fallow Media is an experimental, reader-funded online arts publication which combines modern web technologies with longform writing and multimedia projects to create a truly unique presence in contemporary publishing.

Royal Society of Antiquarians

Housed in an historic building on Dublin's Merrion Square, the RSAI is a venerable institution in Irish historical circles. We developed a fresh new look for their website, and integrated a digital workflow for the societys traditional membership process.

About Us

At154, we build resiliant, scalable, adaptable systems that fit your needs. Websites, apps, digital platforms – we make sure your foundations are strong, so that you can build with confidence for the future.

When you work with us, we take the time to assess what you currently do, help you to discover what you want to do next, and map a journey from here to there. We produce extensive, detailed research about your work, your audience, and your approach. We analyse every aspect of your relationship with that audience, and we develop plans to make that relationship stronger and more meaningful.

From the words you use to the shape of their presentation, design is a vital question at every step of your journey. We believe that beauty and craft can be the most effective ways of telling your story. It is not about following trends – it is about showing the world who you really are.
